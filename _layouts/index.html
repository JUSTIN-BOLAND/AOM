Getting started with Piwik AOM

1. Upload Piwik AOM plugin to your Piwik installation and install its dependencies.
2. Configure API access to your advertising accounts (Google AdWords, Facebook Ads etc.) and activate the plugin.
3. Parametrize all your links at the advertising platforms you use.
4. Lay back and let Piwik AOM gather the data you need for advanced online marketing.
